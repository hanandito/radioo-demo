About

An alternative music radio and archiving platform community-based in Bogor, notably in the electronic music scene. Providing a place for DJs, musicians, and music geeks from all over the world to promote, distribute, and create new connections, in line with the growth of the music ecosystem surrounds them. Presenting various types of music, and the sub-genres in it, also come with the artistic and visuals that represent the environment of our city.​
